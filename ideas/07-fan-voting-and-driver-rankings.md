# Fan Voting And Driver Rankings

## Concept

Add a social layer where users can vote for drivers and see live rankings.

This gives users a lightweight reason to visit even when they are not doing deep analysis.

## Voting Ideas

### Driver Of The Week

Question:

```text
Who impressed you most this race weekend?
```

### Driver Of The Race

Question:

```text
Who had the best race performance?
```

### Favorite Driver

Question:

```text
Who is your favorite driver?
```

### Fun Award Categories

- Most underrated drive
- Best strategy execution
- Biggest heartbreak
- Best comeback
- Best overtake
- Most improved

## Ranking Views

- This race
- This week
- This season
- All time

## Share Cards

After voting, generate a shareable card:

```text
I voted Lando Norris as Driver of the Race.
Current rank: #2
18,420 votes
```

or:

```text
Your vote moved Leclerc closer to P3.
```

## Placement

Show voting after useful analysis, not before.

Possible flow:

1. User analyzes race.
2. App shows insight cards.
3. App asks: "Who impressed you most in this race?"
4. User votes.
5. User sees ranking and share card.

## Why This Works

- Adds repeat engagement.
- Creates social proof.
- Gives social content a destination.
- Makes the product feel alive between races.
