# Monetization Model

## Goal

Make the project sustainable without relying only on donations.

## Free Product

Keep the core race analyzer free enough to drive traffic.

Free features:

- Race overview
- Basic head-to-head comparison
- Results and race context
- Basic telemetry cards
- Beginner explanations
- Limited AI explanations
- Fan voting
- Leaderboards

## Paid Fan Tier

Charge for depth, convenience, and repeated use.

Possible features:

- More AI explanations
- Deep AI race reports
- Saved race notebooks
- Advanced strategy analysis
- Advanced telemetry explanations
- Compare drivers across multiple races
- Season trend analysis
- Downloadable reports
- Ad-free experience

Possible pricing:

```text
Plus: $5-8/month
Race Weekend Pass: $2-3 for one GP weekend
```

The race weekend pass may be easier for casual fans than a subscription.

## Creator Pack

Sell reusable insight assets to F1 content creators.

Possible package:

- Race summary
- Strategy summary
- Driver comparison snippets
- Chart images
- Short-form content prompts
- Caption ideas
- Beginner explainer scripts

This may be easier to monetize early than fan subscriptions.

## Sponsorship And Affiliate

Potential partners:

- F1 merchandise affiliates
- Sim racing gear
- Fantasy F1 tools
- Motorsport newsletters
- Data or analytics education products
- Ticket/travel partners

Be careful with F1 branding and race footage rights. Prefer original charts, original commentary, app screenshots, and legally safe embeds/links.

## Social Funnel

ThrottleLabs social content should drive traffic to the app.

```text
Race insight video
-> Try analyzer
-> Vote for driver
-> See ranking
-> Share result
```

## Suggested Early Monetization Roadmap

1. Build traffic with free race analysis and social content.
2. Add voting and share cards.
3. Add limited AI card explanations.
4. Add paid deep race reports.
5. Add creator packs.
6. Add sponsorship/affiliate once there is measurable traffic.
