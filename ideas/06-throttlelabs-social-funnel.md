# ThrottleLabs Social Funnel

## Concept

Use ThrottleLabs as the social media brand that drives users into the app.

The social content should not be separate from the product. It should be generated from app insights and send users back to the analyzer.

## Funnel

```text
Race happens
-> App generates analysis
-> Best insight becomes short-form content
-> Viewer visits app
-> Viewer analyzes race or driver
-> Viewer votes or shares
-> Ranking/share card spreads further
```

## Content Types

- The 30-second reason the race changed.
- One chart that explains the race.
- Why Driver A beat Driver B.
- Strategy mistake of the weekend.
- Safety car impact explained.
- Undercut vs overcut using this race.
- Beginner F1 lesson from the latest GP.
- What telemetry says about the battle.

## Example Script

```text
Everyone is talking about the winner, but the race changed on lap 23.

The safety car compressed the field, which made the next pit stop window much more important than raw pace.

I built a fun F1 analyzer where you can break down the race, compare drivers, and see what actually happened.

Try it after the race, and before you leave, vote for your Driver of the Week.
```

## Product Integration

Every post should point to a specific app action:

- Try the race analyzer.
- Compare these two drivers.
- Vote for Driver of the Week.
- See the live fan ranking.
- Read the full race breakdown.

## Why This Matters

Social content creates distribution. The app creates deeper engagement. Voting and share cards create repeat loops.
